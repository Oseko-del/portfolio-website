Video Editing

My Journey into Film & Video Editing

I first got into the film industry out of curiosity and a strong interest in how videos are created and edited. What started as watching films and online content quickly turned into a desire to understand the process behind them. I began exploring video editing and slowly developed my skills using tools like Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe After Effects.

Learning the Basics

At the beginning, I focused on learning the basics of cutting clips, arranging scenes, and improving audio and visual flow in Premiere Pro. As I grew more confident, I moved into After Effects, where I learned motion graphics, visual effects, and how to create more dynamic and engaging visuals.

Growing Confidence

Over time, I started combining both tools to produce more professional and creative projects. This journey helped me build not only technical skills but also a creative mindset for storytelling through video. I am still learning and improving every day, aiming to grow further in the film and media industry.

Tools I Use

Adobe Premiere Pro — Professional video editing including cutting, color grading, and audio enhancement for high-quality content.

Adobe After Effects — Creating motion graphics, animations, and visual effects including cinematic transitions and text effects that make videos more engaging.

What's Next

I'm currently expanding into 3D animation and motion design, integrating AI-generated visuals into my workflow. The goal is to produce content that blends traditional editing techniques with modern AI tools to create something truly unique.
